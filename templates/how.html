The Process

How LastPlate Works

Exceptional food should never be wasted. We connect diners with surplus meals and exclusive late-night menus from the city's finest kitchens.

01

Phase One Surplus Rescue

Every night, restaurants prepare slightly more food than necessary to guarantee flawless service. When the evening slows, perfectly crafted dishes remain untouched.

Instead of discarding them, chefs release these meals through LastPlate, allowing guests to discover exceptional food at honest prices while actively reducing waste.

02

Phase Two The Special Menu

Some kitchens go beyond simple surplus. They create a Special LastPlate Menu available exclusively through our platform.

These curated items may include chef experiments, tasting plates, or late-night specials. Think of it as a city-wide secret menu revealed only after hours.

03

Phase Three Seat Reservations
Launching Soon

LastPlate is expanding beyond takeaway. Soon, guests will be able to reserve physical seats at partner restaurants during their quiet, late-night hours.

Enjoy intimate dining experiences, curated menus, and exclusive tasting events—transforming how you discover the midnight city.

04

Phase Four Simple Execution

Browse what’s available tonight, unlock a listing, and reserve your meal securely before the kitchen sells out.

Every order guarantees a premium culinary experience, helps restaurants recover value, and pushes the industry toward zero waste.
